About Earth Day

Earth Day is an annual event celebrated around the world on April 22 to demonstrate support for environmental protection. First celebrated in 1970, it now includes events coordinated globally by EARTHDAY.ORG in more than 193 countries.


Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about environmental issues and inspire people to take action to protect our planet. We believe that everyone can make a difference, no matter how small the action.

Earth Day Activities

Plant a Tree

Planting trees is a great way to help the environment and beautify your community. Get involved in tree planting activities in your area!


Volunteer for Cleanups

Join local cleanups to help remove trash and debris from parks, beaches, and other natural areas. Every little bit helps!


Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Reduce waste by recycling materials like paper, plastic, and glass. You can also reuse items to give them a second life!
