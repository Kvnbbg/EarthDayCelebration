Plant a Tree

Planting trees is a great way to help the environment and beautify your community. Get involved in tree planting activities in your area!


Volunteer for Cleanups

Join local cleanups to help remove trash and debris from parks, beaches, and other natural areas. Every little bit helps!


Recycle and Reuse

Reduce waste by recycling materials like paper, plastic, and glass. You can also reuse items to give them a second life!
